About

Work

The OrderUp delivery tracker was one of the first of its kind in the food delivery space. It answers the question, “Where’s my food?” with a map that pinpoints the driver’s location and updates the customer on the status of their order.

Complete UI makeover on the OrderUp iOS & Android native apps.

The OrderUp native driver app is the home for OrderUp driver partners. In this experience, drivers are able to fulfill deliveries from dispatch to drop off, view earnings on a daily and weekly basis, and manage their schedule.
